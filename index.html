Important notes:

I am not a native ASL user. My fingerspelling technique shouldn't be taken as an example of what-to-do.

This website provides buttons to look-up the fingerspelled word. keep in mind that ASL signs do not (usually) represent english words. for example the sign meaning "to move quickly with ones legs" (run) can not be used to mean "run a store"

American Sign Language is its own natural language with its own words, grammar, culture etc....

for more information about ASL including free lessons I highly reccomend the lessons available for free at

https://www.lifeprint.com/asl101/lessons/lessons.htm
